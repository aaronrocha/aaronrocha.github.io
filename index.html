Ambition

I woke up remembering what I was dreaming about. I was dreaming about trying to recruit one of my family members into a business I had started. I can remember how easily I was able to articulate what I wanted to communicate. But no matteer how good my pitch was, my target was not having any of it.

So I then began to think about what it is to be ambitious. Unfortunately, I woke up with this on my mind. I kept on trying to think about ambition. What is it exactly. One thing I determined is that one must be exited to reach a goal. One has to have a goal and or a dream. Do we all have one?

To try to make this bit short. I thought ambition comes from having a goal and or a dream. I can become attractive to my target if I can discover what this goal of theirs is and help them achieve such aspiration. Then maybe, I can become a good recruiter. What next? I will develop open ended questions to draw out information about what drives (motivates) or what goals and dreams I need to help them achieve.
